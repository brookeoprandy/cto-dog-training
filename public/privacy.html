Privacy Policy — CTO Dog Training

Last updated: July 2026

CTO Dog Training ("the app") is a trainer dashboard used to log dog training sessions, behaviors, files, and progress. This page explains what information the app handles and how.

Information the app stores

When you create a trainer account and use the app, it stores the following directly on your device:

  • Trainer account details you provide (username, display name, phone number, profile photo)
  • Dog records you create (name, breed, age, training goals, owner name and phone number, behavior notes)
  • Session logs, checklists, and photos/videos you upload for each dog

This information is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to or stored on any company server or database operated by us.

AI-generated summaries

If you use the "Generate Summary" feature, the session notes for that specific dog are sent to Anthropic (the maker of the Claude AI model) through a secure proxy, solely to generate a written progress summary. No other data is sent as part of this feature, and this text is not stored by us after the summary is generated.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not collect data beyond what's described above.

Your data

Since information is stored locally on your device, deleting the app or clearing its data will remove all locally stored information. You can also delete individual dogs, files, or your account directly within the app.
