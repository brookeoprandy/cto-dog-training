Support — CTO Dog Training

CTO Dog Training is a trainer dashboard for logging dog training sessions, behaviors, files, and progress.

Need help?

If you're running into a bug, have a question about using the app, or need to request a data change or deletion, email boprandy123@gmail.com and we'll get back to you.

Common questions

I can't log in. Make sure you're using the same username and password you created your account with — accounts and data are stored on your device, so they don't carry over automatically between phones.

My data disappeared. Data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app or clearing its storage will remove locally saved dogs, sessions, and files.

The AI summary isn't working. This feature requires an internet connection. If it still fails, email us at the address above.
